# Merge

The Merge board is part of the power system on Aurora, CUAUVâ€™s 2021-2022 submarine. The board allows the sub to draw power from the battery that has a higher voltage and to function without interruption during battery swaps. It also isolates power into two channels for mechatronics and the rest of the sub.
